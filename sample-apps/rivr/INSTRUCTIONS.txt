1) Dial the access number for the service

a) Listen to the prompt. It will tell you to enter a phone number. You must enter the area code as well, so that the full number is   a ten digit number.

b) You will be asked to enter the mobile number again. Do so.

c) You will be asked, depending on whether you entered a number with a valid area code, how many units you want to load.

d) Next, you will be asked for your debit card account number. Enter your debit card number EXACTLY as shown on the card. Then press the '#' key.

e) You will be asked to enter a pin number. Enter your 4 digit pin number.

7) Wait until you hear a voice. The system will thank you for using the service and then hang up. You will receive a text message with your recharge code.
